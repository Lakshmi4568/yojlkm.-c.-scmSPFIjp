IEEE SB GECT PES refers to the IEEE Power & Energy Society (PES) Student Branch Chapter at Government Engineering College Thrissur (GECT), a local chapter focused on power and energy engineering, providing resources and activities for students.

More Details

What it is:

IEEE:
The Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, a global professional organization for engineers and technologists.
SB:
Student Branch, a local chapter within the IEEE structure that focuses on student activities and networking.
GECT:
Government Engineering College Thrissur, the university where the IEEE Student Branch is located.
PES:
Power & Energy Society, a technical society within IEEE focused on electric power and energy.
Chapter:
A local group within the IEEE PES structure, providing a platform for students to engage with the field of power and energy.
What it does:
Advancing technology:
The IEEE SB GECT PES aims to advance technology for the good of humanity.
Hosting events:
It organizes various national, state, and college-level events and projects to help students build technical skills.
Providing resources:
It provides resources and activities for students interested in power and energy engineering.
Networking:
It facilitates networking opportunities for students with industry professionals and other students.
Supporting student members:
It supports student members in representing the branch in various international events.
High Performing Student Branch Chapter Program (HPSBCP):
PES provides funding to PES Student Branch Chapters (SBCs) to carry out activities supporting their local members through the HPSBCP.
IEEE PES Day:
IEEE PES organizes IEEE PES Day to celebrate the contributions of innovation to new energy ventures and the energy transition.
Key Features:
Multiple Societies:
The IEEE SB GECT hosts multiple societies, including CS, ComSoc, IAS, PES, PELS, SPS, RAS, and TEMS, as well as one Affinity Group (WIE).
Accolades:
The student branch has received accolades, including the Outstanding Student Branch (Kerala Section) award in 2016.
Student-focused:
The chapter is primarily focused on the needs and interests of students in the field of power and energy engineering.
PES Chapters:
PES has 800+ global chapters made up of Professional Chapters and Student Branch Chapters.
Benefits of Membership:
Students enjoy special IEEE and PES membership discounts.
Student Branch Chapter Creation:
To create a PES Student Branch Chapter, an IEEE Student Branch is required, along with signatures of at least six IEEE Student Branch members who are also PES members, a Faculty Advisor who is also a member of IEEE and PES, and a completed and approved petition.

PES Team Members:
